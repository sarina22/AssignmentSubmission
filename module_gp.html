Introduction

The project that we are given is about developing our own website. Making any website is not as easy as it seems . Website is the collection of webpages .It is a hypertext document which is connected to the web so that everyone with the internet connection can view the webpages. In this century , we all get information from the different websites but we do not have enough knowledge about how to make any type of website of our. So, this project helps us to get connected with the rest of the world by helping us to build a website of our own. We are basically given six webpages to make. These pages include the main homepage which is our landing page and contains the front cover for the first (overall) view of the website. The next is ”About” page that includes information about us (who we are, things we like and other things that are related to us).In the page”CV” we are given to make our resume ,our experience and any skills that we have.Since, we all use social medias like facebook, Instagram ,linkedin and other widely used medias , the social media links should be kept in the “Social” page. Furthermore, “ Contact” page contains our location and a way for other people to get connected to us by filling up the form and submitting it online. Finally , last page is about the report of the overall project we are given.

Research and teamwork

Looking the way webpages looked ,it seemed that the webpages were easy to make but then actually it was a challenging task for the beginner . The project was worth it , it was challenging and made me proud to have my own website.I gathered ideas from other websites and tried to create of my own. Actually ended up choosing the simple one for my website because it is easy to learn from the simple and more like a basic one.

Working in a group to discuss the design part and the contents that we should be keeping in our webpages was a challenging as well as a fun part. Since, we did not have enough information and ideas to make the website ,we stumbled upon different others websites from the internet and we made sketches from our overall ideas and information. We worked in a group of five people .At first we studied the given assignment brief clearly and decided to divide the task into color choosing, animation part, design part ,content part and responsive part .
